Qno1.What is SEO and explain the importance of SEO?
Sol : Search engine optimization (SEO) is the art and science of getting pages to rank higher in search engines such as Google. 
      Because search is one of the main ways in which people discover content online, ranking higher in search engines can lead 
      to an increase in traffic to a website.
importance of SEO
Meta tags are important for SEO because they provide information to search engines about the content on your
site and can help improve your website's visibility and ranking in search engine results pages.
